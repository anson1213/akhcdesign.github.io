What We Do

Mach Retail Development Group Ltd who has more than 20 years’ experience, is specialized manufacturing display and props for below area: Fashion Window and Props, Watch and Jewelry, Cosmetic & Fragrance, Eyewear and Alternative Visual Merchandising props with the highest quality offer to our clients. We served International Luxury brands and International Design Agency.

With our Hong Kong Management and our Europe Representative (able to speak Italian and French) in action, we have developed a whole new production system and able to help our clients in below area, such as Timing, Costing, Quality Control and especially Communication.

In order to fulfilled our customer needs, we have also provided installation service. Our Installation team has more than 15 years experiences in working with different APAC countries to install shop furniture and windows.
